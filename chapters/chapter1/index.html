ARTICLE A

Short Title and Scope

Section 1A.01 — Title

This Ordinance shall be known as the CODE OF GENERAL ORDINANCES OF SANTA, ILOCOS SUR.

Explanation

This section formally establishes the official name of the codified municipal ordinance system of Santa, Ilocos Sur. The designation serves as the legal identity of the ordinance code and becomes the official reference used in municipal governance, enforcement, administrative interpretation, and future amendments.

The title also signifies that the ordinance compilation is intended to function as a consolidated municipal legal framework rather than as isolated individual ordinances.

Section 1A.02 — Scope

This code covers all general and special ordinances enacted over the years by the municipality of Santa, Ilocos Sur.

Explanation

This provision defines the coverage and jurisdictional scope of the Code. It clarifies that the codified system includes both general ordinances and special ordinances previously enacted by the Municipality of Santa.

Legally, this means that the Code operates as a centralized repository and governing framework for municipal legislation. The provision ensures organizational continuity and allows existing and future ordinances to be systematically integrated into the codified structure.

ARTICLE B

Rules of Construction

Purpose of Article B

Article B establishes the legal interpretation framework of the entire Code of General Ordinances. These provisions guide how words, phrases, timelines, references, and conflicts between provisions must be understood and applied.

In practice, these rules operate as the municipal statutory interpretation system. They help prevent ambiguity, inconsistencies, and conflicting legal interpretations during implementation and enforcement.

Section 1B.01 — Words and Phrases

Words and phrases embodied in this code but not specifically defined shall have the same meaning as found in legal dictionaries as well as in existing laws.

Explanation

This provision establishes the default interpretative rule for undefined terms. If a word or phrase is not expressly defined within the Code itself, its meaning must be determined using recognized legal dictionaries and existing laws.

This ensures consistency between municipal ordinances and broader Philippine legal principles. It also prevents arbitrary interpretations by requiring reliance on recognized legal standards.

Section 1B.02 — Construction of Codal Provisions

This section establishes the official rules for interpreting codal provisions including grammar, gender, computation of time, references, and conflict resolution.

Explanation

This section serves as one of the most important interpretative provisions in the entire Code because it standardizes how municipal laws must be read and applied.

The rules ensure consistency in legal construction and reduce confusion during ordinance implementation, adjudication, and enforcement.

  • General Rule — words are interpreted according to common and accepted language unless technical meaning is required.
  • Gender and Number — masculine terms apply to all genders, while singular and plural references are interpreted flexibly.
  • Computation of Time — establishes how legal periods and deadlines are calculated.
  • Tenses — clarifies how present and future tense provisions operate.
  • References — confirms that chapter, article, and section references pertain to this Code unless otherwise specified.
  • Conflicting Provisions — determines which provision prevails in cases of conflict.

Section 1B.03 — Amendment and Integration of Additional Provisions

Any amendment on this Code may be introduced to the chapter, article or section.

Explanation

This provision authorizes the continued expansion and updating of the Code through future ordinances and amendments.

It establishes the legal mechanism for integrating new provisions into the codified structure while preserving organizational consistency and legislative continuity.

Section 1B.04 — Existing Rights

Explanation

This provision protects vested rights, ongoing proceedings, and accrued legal interests existing before the effectivity of the Code.

The section prevents retroactive impairment of rights while allowing future procedures to conform with the updated codified framework whenever legally possible.

Section 1B.05 — Effect of Heading

Explanation

This provision clarifies that headings, titles, and labels are merely organizational tools and do not control or alter the legal meaning of substantive provisions.

The actual controlling authority remains the text of the ordinance provision itself.

Section 1B.06 — Relation to Prior Ordinances

Explanation

This provision clarifies that substantially similar provisions from previous ordinances are considered restatements rather than entirely new enactments.

Its purpose is to preserve legal continuity and avoid unintended disruption of existing municipal legal principles.

ARTICLE C

Definitions

Purpose of Article C

Article C establishes the official technical and legal definitions used throughout the Code of General Ordinances.

These definitions standardize interpretation, reduce ambiguity, and provide the conceptual framework necessary for ordinance enforcement and administration.

Business

A trade or commercial activity regularly engaged in as a means of livelihood involving independent judgment and decision-making.

License or Permit

A legal authorization granted by competent authority allowing a person or entity to engage in regulated activity or occupation.

Tax

An enforced monetary contribution imposed by law to support governmental operations and public services.

Profession

A calling requiring the passing of government board or bar examinations such as law, medicine, engineering, and accountancy.

Motor Vehicle

A vehicle propelled by power other than muscular force and intended for public road usage subject to statutory exclusions.

Revenue

Taxes, fees, and charges collected by the government for public purposes and governmental operations.
