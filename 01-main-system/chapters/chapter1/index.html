GOVERNANCE / REVENUE ACTIVE TERM

Business

A trade or commercial activity regularly engaged in as means of livelihood involving independent judgment and decision-making.

Municipal Context

Businesses operating within Santa may become subject to permits, taxation, inspections, fees, and commercial regulation.

LICENSING SYSTEM REGULATORY TERM

License or Permit

A legal authorization granted by competent authority allowing regulated activity or occupation.

Municipal Context

Permits and licenses function as regulatory mechanisms used by the municipality to supervise lawful operations and maintain ordinance compliance.

REVENUE SYSTEM FISCAL TERM

Tax

An enforced monetary contribution imposed by law to support governmental operations and public services.

Municipal Context

Municipal taxes support governance operations, infrastructure, sanitation services, enforcement systems, and community programs.

PROFESSIONAL SYSTEM REGULATED TERM

Profession

A calling requiring government board or bar examinations such as law, medicine, engineering, and accountancy.

Municipal Context

Professionals operating within the municipality may become subject to permits, taxation, and regulatory requirements.

TRANSPORTATION ENFORCEMENT TERM

Motor Vehicle

A vehicle propelled by power other than muscular force intended for public road usage subject to statutory exclusions.

Municipal Context

Motor vehicles may become subject to traffic regulation, transportation ordinances, parking control, and public safety enforcement.

FISCAL GOVERNANCE REVENUE TERM

Revenue

Taxes, fees, and charges collected by government for public purposes and governmental operations.

Municipal Context

Municipal revenue supports governance operations, infrastructure, enforcement systems, public services, and administrative functions.
