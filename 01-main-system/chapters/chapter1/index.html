Article A — Short Title and Scope

This Ordinance shall be known as the CODE OF GENERAL ORDINANCES OF SANTA, ILOCOS SUR.

Explanation

This section establishes the official legal identity of the codified municipal ordinance system of Santa, Ilocos Sur. The title becomes the formal reference used for municipal governance, legislative interpretation, ordinance enforcement, and future codification.

It also signifies that the municipality operates under an organized and consolidated legal framework rather than isolated ordinance enactments.

This code covers all general and special ordinances enacted over the years by the municipality of Santa, Ilocos Sur.

Explanation

This provision defines the jurisdictional scope and legal coverage of the Code of General Ordinances. It clarifies that both general and special municipal ordinances enacted by the Municipality of Santa are integrated into the codified system.

The provision ensures continuity of municipal legislation while establishing the Code as the centralized legal repository of the municipality.

Article B — Rules of Construction

Article B establishes the interpretative framework governing the construction, application, and legal understanding of the Code of General Ordinances.

Words and phrases embodied in this code but not specifically defined shall have the same meaning as found in legal dictionaries as well as in existing laws.

Explanation

This provision establishes the default interpretative rule for undefined legal terms appearing in the Code.

When a word or phrase is not expressly defined within the ordinance itself, its meaning must be determined using recognized legal dictionaries and applicable laws.

This rule prevents arbitrary interpretation and promotes consistency with Philippine legal standards and statutory interpretation principles.

Legal Importance

This section serves as one of the most important interpretative provisions in the entire municipal code because it standardizes how ordinance provisions must be read, understood, and applied.

  • General Rule — words are interpreted according to common legal usage unless technical meaning is required.
  • Gender and Number — masculine terms include all genders while singular and plural forms are interpreted flexibly.
  • Computation of Time — establishes how legal periods, procedural timelines, and ordinance deadlines are calculated.
  • Tenses — present tense provisions may include future application whenever necessary for legal interpretation.
  • References — chapter, article, and section references are interpreted as references to this Code unless otherwise stated.
  • Conflicting Provisions — determines which ordinance provision prevails in case of legal conflict.

Municipal Context

These construction rules guide municipal officials, enforcement personnel, administrators, and future legal interpretation of the Code.

Without these rules, ordinance implementation could become inconsistent or legally ambiguous.

Any amendment on this Code may be introduced to the chapter, article or section.

Explanation

This provision authorizes the continued updating and expansion of the Code through future amendments, ordinances, and codification revisions.

It establishes the legal mechanism allowing future municipal legislation to integrate into the existing codified framework while preserving organizational consistency.

Article C — Definitions

Article C establishes the official technical and legal definitions used throughout the Code of General Ordinances.

Business

A trade or commercial activity regularly engaged in as means of livelihood involving independent judgment and decision-making.

Municipal Context

Businesses operating within the Municipality of Santa may be subject to permits, taxation, inspections, fees, and municipal regulation.

License or Permit

A legal authorization granted by competent authority allowing regulated activity or occupation.

Municipal Context

Permits and licenses are essential regulatory tools used by the municipality to supervise lawful activities and maintain compliance with local regulations.

Tax

An enforced monetary contribution imposed by law to support governmental operations and public services.

Municipal Context

Municipal taxes support infrastructure, governance operations, public services, sanitation, enforcement, and other local government functions.

Profession

A calling requiring government board or bar examinations such as law, medicine, engineering, and accountancy.

Municipal Context

Professionals operating within the municipality may be subject to professional taxation, permits, or local business regulations depending on applicable ordinances.

Motor Vehicle

A vehicle propelled by power other than muscular force and intended for public road usage subject to statutory exclusions.

Municipal Context

Motor vehicles may become subject to municipal traffic rules, parking regulations, transportation ordinances, and public safety enforcement.

Revenue

Taxes, fees, and charges collected by government for public purposes and governmental operations.

Municipal Context

Municipal revenue serves as the financial foundation supporting local governance, enforcement operations, public infrastructure, and community services.
