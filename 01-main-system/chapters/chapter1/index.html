Article A — Short Title and Scope

This Ordinance shall be known as the CODE OF GENERAL ORDINANCES OF SANTA, ILOCOS SUR.

Explanation

This section establishes the official legal identity of the codified municipal ordinance system of Santa, Ilocos Sur.

The title becomes the formal legal reference used for municipal governance, legislative interpretation, ordinance enforcement, and codification.

This code covers all general and special ordinances enacted over the years by the municipality of Santa, Ilocos Sur.

Explanation

This provision defines the legal coverage and jurisdictional scope of the Code of General Ordinances.

The Code serves as the centralized repository of municipal legislation enacted by the Municipality of Santa.

Article B — Rules of Construction

Article B establishes the interpretative framework governing the legal construction, application, and understanding of the Code of General Ordinances.

Words and phrases embodied in this code but not specifically defined shall have the same meaning as found in legal dictionaries as well as in existing laws.

Explanation

Undefined legal terms must be interpreted using recognized legal dictionaries and existing laws to maintain legal consistency and prevent arbitrary interpretation.

Legal Interpretation Rules

  • General Rule — words interpreted according to common legal usage.
  • Gender and Number — masculine references include all genders and singular/plural forms.
  • Computation of Time — establishes legal timelines and deadlines.
  • Tenses — present tense may include future application.
  • Conflicting Provisions — determines which ordinance prevails in legal conflict.

Any amendment on this Code may be introduced to the chapter, article or section.

Explanation

This provision authorizes future amendments, additions, revisions, and codification updates within the existing ordinance structure.

Article C — Definitions

Article C establishes the official legal and technical definitions used throughout the Code of General Ordinances.

A trade or commercial activity regularly engaged in as means of livelihood involving independent judgment and decision-making.

Municipal Context

Businesses operating within Santa may become subject to permits, taxation, inspections, fees, and commercial regulation.

A legal authorization granted by competent authority allowing regulated activity or occupation.

Municipal Context

Permits and licenses are regulatory mechanisms used by the municipality to supervise lawful operations and maintain ordinance compliance.

Municipal Context

Municipal taxes support governance operations, infrastructure, sanitation services, enforcement systems, and community programs.

Municipal Context
