Article A — Short Title and Scope

This article establishes the official legal identity, title, scope, and codified jurisdiction of the Code of General Ordinances of Santa, Ilocos Sur.

This Ordinance shall be known as the CODE OF GENERAL ORDINANCES OF SANTA, ILOCOS SUR.

This code covers all general and special ordinances enacted over the years by the Municipality of Santa, Ilocos Sur.

Codified Municipal Authority

The Code serves as the centralized repository of municipal ordinances governing local administration, legislative authority, and municipal regulatory systems.

Article B — Rules of Construction

This article governs legal interpretation, codal construction, municipal legislative application, and rules governing undefined terminology within the ordinance system.

Words and phrases embodied in this code but not specifically defined shall have the same meaning as found in legal dictionaries as well as in existing laws.

Legal Interpretation Rule

Undefined legal terminology shall be interpreted using recognized legal usage, existing laws, and accepted legal dictionaries to preserve consistency within ordinance implementation.

  • Words shall be understood according to legal usage.
  • Masculine gender includes feminine references.
  • Singular terms may include plural forms.
  • Present tense may include future application.
  • Conflicting provisions shall be interpreted harmoniously.

Any amendment on this Code may be introduced to the chapter, article, or section.

Legislative Continuity

This provision authorizes future legislative amendments, codification updates, and integration of additional ordinances within the municipal legal system.

Article C — Definitions

This article establishes official municipal legal terminology, technical definitions, and interpretative classifications used throughout the Code of General Ordinances.

Business

A trade or commercial activity regularly engaged in as means of livelihood involving independent judgment and decision-making.

License or Permit

A legal authorization granted by competent authority allowing regulated activity or occupation.

Tax

An enforced monetary contribution imposed by law for public governmental purposes.

Profession

A calling requiring government board or bar examinations such as law, medicine, engineering, or accountancy.

Motor Vehicle

A vehicle propelled by power other than muscular force for public road transportation.

Revenue

Taxes, fees, and charges collected for governmental operations and municipal services.
