ARTICLE A

Declaration of Policy

Public office is a public trust. Officials shall serve with integrity, accountability, loyalty, efficiency, and professionalism.

ARTICLE B

Norms of Conduct

Officials shall prioritize public welfare over personal interest.

Public officials shall demonstrate competence, dedication, and fairness.

ARTICLE C

Duties & Responsibilities

Municipal officials shall perform their duties with accountability, efficiency, and dedication to public service.

ARTICLE D

Ethical Standards

Public officials shall avoid financial and personal interests that conflict with public duties.

Acceptance of gifts, favors, or benefits that may influence official acts is prohibited.

Confidential government information shall not be used for private advantage or unauthorized disclosure.

ARTICLE E

Transparency

Officials shall file declarations of assets, liabilities, and financial interests in accordance with law.

Records and official transactions shall remain accessible subject to lawful confidentiality.

Municipal offices shall promote open, transparent, and accountable governance.

ARTICLE F

Conflict of Interest

Officials shall divest from business interests that create conflicts with public office.

Outside employment that interferes with official duties is prohibited.

ARTICLE G

Penalties

Violations may result in disciplinary action, suspension, dismissal, or other sanctions.

All disciplinary proceedings shall observe fairness and due process.

ARTICLE H

Department Heads

The Municipal Treasurer shall take custody of municipal funds, maintain financial records, and supervise revenue collection.

The Municipal Assessor shall ensure proper appraisal and assessment of real properties.

The Municipal Engineer shall supervise infrastructure, public works, and engineering projects.

The Municipal Health Officer shall implement health programs, sanitation systems, and medical services.

ARTICLE I

Travel Orders

Travel orders shall be approved by authorized officials before any official travel.

Travel expenses and reports shall comply with auditing rules and accountability standards.

ARTICLE J

Mandatory Drug Testing

Municipal officials and employees shall undergo mandatory drug testing in accordance with applicable laws and regulations.

Drug test results shall remain confidential and handled only by authorized personnel.

Officials testing positive may undergo rehabilitation, administrative proceedings, or sanctions pursuant to law.

ARTICLE K

Legal Assistance Fund

The Legal Assistance Fund shall provide legal support to municipal officials and employees for acts performed in official duties.

Funding shall come from lawful appropriations and authorized sources subject to accounting and auditing rules.

ARTICLE L

Public Employment Service Office

The PESO shall facilitate employment opportunities, job matching, and labor referrals.

The office shall maintain employment databases and labor market information systems.

Programs supporting youth employment and livelihood opportunities shall be implemented.

ARTICLE M

People’s Law Enforcement Board

The PLEB shall hear and decide citizen complaints against members of the police force within its jurisdiction.

The Board shall consist of representatives from the community, local government, and accredited sectors.

Funding and compensation shall be subject to municipal appropriations and applicable auditing regulations.

ARTICLE N

Official Seal Ordinance

The Official Seal shall be used only for authorized municipal transactions, documents, communications, and legal instruments.

Proper custody and preservation of the seal shall be maintained by authorized municipal personnel.

Unauthorized reproduction, misuse, or alteration of the Official Seal shall be penalized in accordance with law and municipal ordinances.
